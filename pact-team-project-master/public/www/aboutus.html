About Our Website!

This website is for Delicious Factory. Delicious Factory is a restaurant located right near Arizona State University: Tempe campus off of Mill Avenue. Within our website, we have a contact page that includes links to the social media that Delicious Factory is part of. Also, this site contains a menu page that lists various items that Delicious Factory along with images of their products. Finally, we have a page that is identical to the menu page, but allows users to also add orders to a cart, which calculates a total amount of an order for the user.
